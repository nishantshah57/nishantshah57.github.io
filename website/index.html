About Me

Hello, I am Nishant Shah!
(Sage of Six Paths)

I was a Robotics Software Engineer at Humatics, Waltham, MA. I was an Robotics Software Intern at Humatics in Summer 2019 and an Algorithms Intern at Philips in Summer 2018.

I have completed my MS in Robotics Engineering from Worcester Polytechnic Institute , MA, USA in 2019 and BE in Mechatronics Engineering from G H Patel College of Engineering and Technology , Gujarat, India in 2016.

My work at Humatics revolved around developing new algorithms, implementing API's in C++, integrating third party libraries and packages for robot localization, testing and analyzing proprietary sensor data like Ultrawideband, IMU and odometry writing Python scripts. I was also responsible for maintaining in-house navigation module to test and analyze the localization module performance and maintaining CI/CD pipelines using Docker.

I have an interest in the field of software development, state estimation, motion planning, perception, backend engineering, SLAM, reinforcement learning, navigation, manipulators and mobile robotics.
